Welcome To Your Dental Care

A genuine smile comes from the heart, but a healthy smile needs good dental care.

Teleconsultation

Dentists can offer virtual consultations where patients can discuss their dental concerns and symptoms through video calls. Dentists can provide advice, guidance, and preliminary diagnoses.

Remote Monitoring

For certain dental conditions, patients can use other devices to capture images or videos of their oral health and send them to their dentists through the mobile app. Dentists can then review the materials remotely and monitor the progress of treatment or evaluate any concerns.

Oral Health Education

Mobile Application can provide educational resources such as articles, videos, and interactive tools to help patients understand dental hygiene practices, preventive care, and treatment options.

Appointment Booking

Patients can schedule appointments through online mobile app. This streamlines the process and provides convenience for both patients and dental clinics.

Follow-up Care

Dentists can conduct virtual follow-up sessions with patients to assess treatment progress, answer questions, and provide additional guidance.

Emergency Consultations

In case of dental emergencies, online consultations can help assess the situation, provide temporary relief measures, and determine whether an in-person visit is necessary.
